My Web Hosting Pick

Based on the research that I have done on the web hosting services provided by Host Gator, Go Daddy, and Blue Host, I have determined a hosting company that I would like to work with if I were to start a new website. My pick is to use the web hosting services of Go Daddy. One reason why I picked Go Daddy is because overall their site seemed to be much easier to get information from, which as a potential customer is critical. Besides the personal factors of why I picked Go Daddy, I liked the fact that their packages all included some sort of access to social media and email features. As a business in today's day and age, it is critical to have a digital presence including a website, social media, and email. Another factor that drew me to Go Daddy was the fact that for only $25 a month, I have full access to all of the basic, guidance and analytics, social media and email marketing, online appointment, and eCommerce features. The difference of roughly $12 dollars from the highest package offered by Blue Host to that of the highest Go Daddy package will not make or break a business (at least I hope not). Another factor that was playing in my mind when looking at these providers is the common saying, "You get what you pay for." Yes, the eCommerce package from Go Daddy is the most expensive out of the three providers, however, I believe that this additional cost comes with enough extra's to make it worth it
